Re-create

CROWN Re-create Booklet | CROWN Mentor Guide

We reject the incessant demands of modern life, and seek to model something better. We recognise the importance of having regular rest each week, to “re-create” by involving God in times when we cease “productive” work.

A Re-Create rhythm shows we won’t let the world dictate our pace.

“Then God blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on it he rested from all the work of creating he had done.” (Genesis 2.3)
