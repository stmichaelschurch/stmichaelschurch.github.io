SHORT MENTOR BOOKLETS

These booklets are focused on each specific aspect of the CROWN Rhythm. They are written from the experience of mentoring others in these pattern to help those in triplets or working with groups, or mentees. By identifying common blockages, and including workable next steps, they provide pointers to help in enabling the formation of individual CROWN rules that are sustainable life-giving and fruitful.

DISCIPLING TRIPLETS
