Part 1 - The Call to Discipleship

What is a rule of life, and how might forming one help us?

This first series of short talks identifies why it is important to be intentional about our growth as disciples and how God wants us to be positioned for growth and fruitfulness. We look at how the CROWN framework can be tool that can lead us into the maturity God desires for each us.

Part 2 - Who do we think we are? Identity matters

Why CROWN?

The choice of CROWN for our framework is no accident. It is a statement of who we really are. This second series of short talks explores our identity as Christians and how that should influence the choices we make and the life we lead. Rather than finding our identity in what we do, knowing who we are in Christ, should help us shape a lifestyle that overflows with God’s goodness to others.

Part 3 - Cross & Crown: Rhythms built upon Grace

Can we still live by grace and not law, with a “rule” of life?

CROWN is a framework through which we can shape our lives in response to the truth of scripture and the leading of the Holy Spirit. This third series of short talks seeks to guard against the pit-falls: We won’t find life through striving, nor will be grow if we see achieving our rule as a measure of Spiritual success. Instead, with our focus on the cross, and as we die to ourselves, we will experience more of Jesus’ risen life in the patterns we form.
